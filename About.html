About

My name is David Gray and I am photographer based in Richmond,VA. The combinaton of my love of exploration and my passion for photography prompts me to photograph during my ventures around the globe. Along the way I seek out landscapes,streetscapes, people, and atmospheres that produce a storytelling effect. When these moments speak to me, and if I am lucky enough to have my mamiya c330, Fuji 35mm, or my canon 5d markV with me: I stop, steady the adrenaline rush, open the shutter to the window of opportunity and let the light cook up something beautiful. Sometimes I hope to produce something for a wall in a room, often I frame a message I wish to reveal, and always it's for the love of photography. Till next time. Thanks for looking and sharing your time. Don't be a stranger now.
